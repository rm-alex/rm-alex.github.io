About

Hello! I'm Alex Jonic, and I am a game developer.

I've had a fascination with video games ever since I was young.
Some of my earliest childhood memories consist of watching
my dad play games like Tomb Raider and King's Quest.
Since then, I've grown to see video games as art,
and their development as a creative medium.

I graduated from Lewis University in the Spring of 2019 with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science.

In my down time, I work as a freelance digital artist,
and can often be found working on my own personal video game
projects using Unity and Unreal Engine.
